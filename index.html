Steve Nash

Steve Nash is a former NBA Player that is currently the Head Coach of the Brooklyn Nets. He is considered one of the greatest Point Guards in NBA history. Steve Nash played in the NBA for 19 years.

Facts About Steve Nash

  • Steve Nash was a Canadian basketball player before he joined the NBA.
